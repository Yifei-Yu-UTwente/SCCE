2. Research

Starting in 2025, I continue my research in Circular Economy as a senior researcher at the University of Twente. I focus on the European Project of From Industrial Symbiosis to Hubs for Circularity.

In this project, I extend my scope to the overall circular transition in different industries. Taking the construction industry as an anchor industry, I aim to discover new circular opportunities in different sectors. But this is not easy. We need to carefully manage materials, money, and people around new opportunities. Therefore, many interesting and focused research topics are included. I am here to help you if your organisations need any research support or advice.

Want to know more? Read it here: https://is2h4c-project.eu/

3. Education

I also take the role of a young teacher to educate future talents at the university. The courses are Circular Sustainable Business Development and Circular Economy Transition. I help students to learn circularity basics by playing educational games. Soon, more students will work on their graduation projects in sustainability and circularity. Maybe we can find a nice collaboration together with you and students.
